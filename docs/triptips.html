Trip Tips for Visitors

Travel Tips

When traveling in the DRC, plan ahead and stay informed. Explore safely with local guides, respect cultural traditions, and be prepared for diverse experiences.

Language tips

you can learn some work in local Language like Hello, i am new, and thank you

  • Hello = “Mbote” (Lingala) "Jambo"(Swahili)
  • Thank you = “Melesi” (Lingala) / “aksanti” (Swahili)
  • I am new here = “Naza ya sika awa” (Lingala) / "niko wa sasa uku"(Swahili)
  • Goodbye = “Kende malamu” (Lingala) / "kwa eri" (Swahili)
